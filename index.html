Your Name

Kimi no Nawa.

Your Name (Kimi no Nawa) is an anime film about two highschool students, Taki and Mitsuha,
who mysteriously being swapping bodies despite not knowing each other and living in different parts of Japan.
As they live each other's lives, the form a deep connection. However, came the time that they stopped switching bodies,
Taki sets out to find Mitsuha, only to be met with a shocking truth.

Characters

Taki Tachibana: A highschool boy living in Tokyo, balancing his school life with a part-time job at a restaurant.
Mitsuha Miyamizu: A highschool girl from a small rual town that wishes to escape her quiet life for Tokyo.
